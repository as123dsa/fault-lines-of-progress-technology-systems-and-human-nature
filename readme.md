## 📖 Overview

This repository serves as a systemic archive and analytical framework for understanding the deep-seated tensions shaping contemporary society. Spanning sociology, political economy, technological philosophy, and cultural criticism, the collection unpacks how **structural paradoxes**—from algorithmic governance and labor precarity to institutional inertias—are redefining the boundaries of human agency, justice, and progress in the mid-2020s.

The common thread across these documents is a critical examination of *systems*: how they are built, how they break, and how they perpetuate "innocent" yet entrenched forms of exclusion, exploitation, and cognitive distortion.
